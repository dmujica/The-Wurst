IT'S THE WURST.

At the Wursthaus all of our recipes in use have been passed down from our Great Grandmother who migrated from Germany in 1897. We like tradition so when it comes to choosing our sources for produce and meat we like to use local sources. All of our pork and beef is farm raise, antibiotic and hormone free. All of our kraut is fermented in our restaurant so you can experience the unique and wonderful flavor of traditional german cooking.
